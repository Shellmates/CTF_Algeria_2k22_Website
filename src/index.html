Description of the clubs

Shellmates

/* where the is a shell, there is a way*/

Shellmates is a group of students passionate about information security. Our objective is creating an InfoSec community, to motivate and encourage knowledge exchange in this field.

Ingeniums

create you own infinity

Ingeniums is a group of students from the Highest Institute of computer science of Sidi Bel Abbès who aspires to create a dynamic environment between students, have enriched exchanges and promote the culture of sharing. And this in an atmosphere of positivity and creativity.
